## How Does BetterHelp Work in Australia?

BetterHelp works the same way in Australia as it does in the US and UK:

1. **You sign up and fill out a questionnaire** about what you're looking for
2. **BetterHelp matches you** with a licensed Australian therapist
3. **You communicate via messaging** (anytime), live chat, phone calls, or video sessions
4. **You can switch therapists** at any time, for any reason

There's no referral from a GP required. No waiting lists. No commute.

## Who Is BetterHelp Best For?

BetterHelp is a strong choice if you:

- Want **flexibility** — therapy on your schedule, not a therapist's
- Prefer **text-based communication** and don't want to do video calls
- Live in a **regional area** with limited local therapist access
- Want to **switch therapists easily** if the fit isn't right
- Have a **moderate budget** — it's more affordable than private in-person therapy

## What BetterHelp Doesn't Do

It's important to be clear about limitations:

- **It's not crisis support.** If you're in crisis, call Lifeline on **13 11 14**.
- **It's not bulk-billed.** You can't use Medicare or a Mental Health Care Plan here.
- **Therapist quality varies.** As with any platform, some therapists are better than others — but switching is free and easy.
