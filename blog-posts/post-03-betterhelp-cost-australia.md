## Can You Get a Medicare Rebate with BetterHelp?

**No.** BetterHelp is a private platform and does not currently work with Medicare or the Medicare Safety Net. This is one of the main downsides compared to in-person therapy.

However — for many people, the cost of BetterHelp still works out cheaper than in-person therapy without a Mental Health Care Plan, especially when you factor in:
- No travel time
- No taking time off work
- No waitlists
